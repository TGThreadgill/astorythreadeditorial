What I Offer

Available Services

Developmental Editing

Comprehensive manuscript assessment and guidance aimed at enhancing overall structure, content coherence, and thematic development. My goal is to improve overall clarity and impact, ensuring that your work resonates effectively with your target audience.

Copyediting

Detailed in-line comments aimed at refining your manuscript — ensuring grammatical accuracy, style consistency, vivid prose, light fact-checking, and adherence to applicable publishing guidelines. Your voice, polished.

Beta / ARC Reading

A first look at unreleased manuscripts from a reader's perspective — gauging whether the story connects with intended audiences. As your ARC reader, I provide early review, build buzz around your new title, and help create visibility through online reviews.

The Editor

About Me

Tarah Threadgill

Developmental Editor & Copyeditor

As a developmental and copy editor, I collaborate with authors to bring their manuscripts to life. I provide services and feedback on narrative structure, plot, pacing, product design, fidelity to CMOS guidelines, and research on market trends. I also conduct background research to ensure the accuracy and quality of each title.

With years of editorial experience, I honed my skills working with diverse genres, styles, and voices. I believe in the power of words — and that the right ones in the right order can mend or break a heart.

I am also a secondary English teacher with over a decade of teaching various courses. I gained valuable skills in project management, leadership, team building, communication, curriculum development, instructional design, assessment writing, and data analysis.

My mission is to inspire and empower students and writers to express themselves creatively and confidently.

MFA, Creative Writing — Old Dominion University MA, Secondary Education — Georgia College & State University
